Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

This privacy policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use our Wear OS watch face application (the "App"). We take your privacy seriously and are committed to transparency about our data practices.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data. The App is a watch face built using the Watch Face Format (WFF), a declarative XML format that runs entirely on your device. It contains no executable code, no analytics frameworks, no advertising SDKs, and no network connectivity.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any personal information to third parties for any purpose.

Complications

The App may display data from built-in Wear OS complications (such as date, battery level, or step count). This data is provided by the Wear OS operating system and remains on your device. We do not access, intercept, or transmit any complication data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, libraries, or APIs that collect user data. Distribution of the App through the Google Play Store is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Data Security

Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no user data at risk. The App operates entirely on-device with no remote servers or cloud infrastructure.

Data Retention and Deletion

We do not retain any user data. Uninstalling the App removes all associated files from your device. There is no account creation, no cloud storage, and no residual data to delete.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. Because we collect no personal data, these rights are satisfied by default. If you have questions about your rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
